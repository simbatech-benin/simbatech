ABOUT

We Are Experts in E-learning

The SIMBA Technology Incubation Center, also known as Simba Tech, is a community-driven platform dedicated to promoting technological innovation, education, and entrepreneurship. Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the tech industry.
The CEO of SIMBA Technology is OKUNGBOWA PETER ODION & OKUNGBOWA PAUL OVBOKHAN . Mr. PETER and PAUL has been involved in the education sector for over 20 years and has successfully built the largest lecture center for students throughout Benin City, as well as a well-recognized secondary school and a primary/nursery school in Benin City, Edo State, Nigeria.
Recognizing the significance and potential of technology, Mr. Paul felt it was essential to establish this technology incubation center.

At SIMBA Tech, our mission is to empower individuals and communities with the skills, knowledge, and resources needed to thrive in the digital age.
